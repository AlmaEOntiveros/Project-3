Purpose: Study how the top 5 stocks in the tech sector performed in the first 100 days of both President Trump and President Biden. Using visualizations created by our team, we explored stock price performance (both foreign and domestic), location, and differences in diversity from 2017 to 2021.

Methods: Using the Python module yfinance, Team One imported stock market data for the following stocks and created visualizations that show the differences in market performance.

Additionally, due to current conditions, Team One explored how diversity in these companies was distributed during this time.

Domestic Stocks

  1. Apple
  2. Nvidia
  3. Microsoft
  4. Amazon
  5. Google(Alphabet)

Foreign Stocks

  1. Samsung, South Korea
  2. ASML, Netherlands
  3. Tencent Holdings, China
  4. Alibaba, China
  5. Sony, Japan
